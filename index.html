Cappuccino

A cappuccino is the perfect balance of espresso, steamed milk and foam.


                      

Java Chip Frappuccino

A blend of Frappuccino chips with coffee, milk and ice that is topped off with whipped cream and a mocha drizzle.

                      

Double Chocolate Chip Frappuccino

The Double Chocolate Chip Frappuccino is made from ice, milk, crème Frappuccino syrup, whipped cream, mocha sauce, and Frappuccino chips.

                      

Caffè Americano

A Caffè Americano combines freshly pulled shots of espresso with hot water to achieve the size of a standard cup of brewed coffee.

                      

Mocha Coffee

A shot of espresso is combined with a chocolate powder, followed by milk or cream.

This recipe requires the heavenly delight combination of vanilla, chocolate, milk, cream, and even an intense shot of coffee.
